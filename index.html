Our story

A one-room studio that never got much bigger, on purpose.

Pressed & Bloom started as a folding table at a Saturday market and a promise: nothing pre-made, nothing shipped in from a warehouse. Ten years later it's still one small studio, still one florist per order, still a folding table for the overflow on our busiest weeks.

We work almost entirely with growers within a few hours of the city, which means our catalog changes with what's actually blooming — not what's convenient to import. If a flower isn't at its best, it isn't in the shop that week.
